# Product Career Ops - Product Philosophy

## Two loops, one career system

Job search and professional development are usually treated as separate activities. That creates a weak feedback system: development is disconnected from market evidence, while job search relies on stale stories and positioning.

Product Career Ops treats them as two loops sharing the same underlying evidence.

### Development loop

The development loop improves:

- product judgment
- leadership capability
- decision quality
- self-awareness
- evidence of impact
- ability to work critically with AI

### Search loop

The search loop tests:

- which mandates fit the user's trajectory
- which evidence resonates
- where level or positioning is unclear
- which competencies the market rewards
- which gaps require real development rather than better wording

Search outcomes become development inputs. Development outputs become stronger search evidence.

## Agent-first, script-controlled

Career work is interpretive. The system therefore uses an AI agent for research, synthesis, coaching, and writing.

Career records require consistency. The system therefore uses deterministic scripts for:

- immutable IDs
- controlled statuses
- score calculations
- workbook mutations
- backups and audit history
- document assembly
- privacy exports

The agent is the conversational control plane. Scripts are the state-control plane.

## AI-in-the-loop coaching

Inspired by Shreyas Doshi's publicly described AI-in-the-loop coaching method, this system treats the model's first response as material for critique rather than an answer to accept.

The coaching objective is to:

- build the habit of evaluating AI output critically
- surface options that may not arise organically
- improve question framing
- combine model breadth with contextual human judgment
- strengthen confidence in the user's own reasoning

The implementation is an original workflow adapted to product leadership and career development.

## Evidence before narrative

Strong career narratives are assembled from credible evidence. The system preserves concrete decisions, actions, metrics, trade-offs, feedback, and reflections before generating polished documents.

No document may contain a claim that cannot be traced to the evidence bank or explicit user confirmation.

## Selective search

The system optimizes for a small number of high-conviction opportunities. A role's title matters less than its mandate, decision authority, scope, trajectory, product quality, and practical fit.

An 80/100 score activates an opportunity. Lower-scoring evaluated roles are archived with reasons, preserving learning and preventing repeated rediscovery.
