# Books

Read foundational titles slowly and return to them. Role-specific titles can be read
just-in-time when a mandate or gap calls for them.

## Product operating model & discovery (foundational)

- **Inspired** — Marty Cagan. How strong product teams build products customers love.
- **Empowered** — Marty Cagan & Chris Jones. Coaching, hiring, and leading product teams.
- **Transformed** — Marty Cagan (SVPG). Moving an organization to the product operating model.
- **Continuous Discovery Habits** — Teresa Torres. Weekly customer touchpoints and the opportunity solution tree.
- **Escaping the Build Trap** — Melissa Perri. Outcome-oriented product organizations.

## Strategy

- **Good Strategy / Bad Strategy** — Richard Rumelt. Diagnosis, guiding policy, coherent action.
- **Crossing the Chasm** — Geoffrey Moore. Bringing disruptive products to mainstream markets.
- **Playing to Win** — A.G. Lafley & Roger Martin. Strategy as a cascade of choices.
- **The Lean Product Playbook** — Dan Olsen. Problem-space to solution-space, product-market fit.

## Leadership & organization

- **The Making of a Manager** — Julie Zhuo. First-principles people management.
- **An Elegant Puzzle** — Will Larson. Systems of engineering/product organization design.
- **Radical Candor** — Kim Scott. Caring personally while challenging directly.
- **Crucial Conversations** — Patterson et al. High-stakes dialogue.
- **The Advantage** — Patrick Lencioni. Organizational health and executive teams.

## Execution, metrics & marketing

- **Outcomes Over Output** — Josh Seiden. Defining and measuring outcomes.
- **Measure What Matters** — John Doerr. OKRs in practice.
- **Radical Focus** — Christina Wodtke. OKRs as a working habit.
- **Lean Analytics** — Alistair Croll & Benjamin Yoskovitz. The right metric for your stage.
- **LOVED** — Martina Lauchengco (SVPG). Modern product marketing.
- **Product Roadmaps Relaunched** — Lombardo, McCarthy, Wang, Price. Roadmaps as strategy, not feature lists.

## Hardware, platforms & AI

- **Build** — Tony Fadell. Building hardware products, teams, and companies.
- **Working Backwards** — Colin Bryar & Bill Carr. Amazon's PR/FAQ and operating mechanisms.
- **The AI Product Playbook** — Oji & Ezinne Udezue. Practical AI product management.
- **Designing Machine Learning Systems** — Chip Huyen. Production ML system design (for PMs partnering with ML teams).

## How to choose

- New to the operating model → *Inspired* then *Empowered*.
- Leading a transformation → *Transformed* + *Good Strategy / Bad Strategy*.
- Weak discovery muscle → *Continuous Discovery Habits*.
- Stepping into executive scope → *Empowered* + *The Advantage*.

Sources: [Ken Norton's book list](https://www.bringthedonuts.com/essays/books-for-product-managers.html), [ProdPad 2025 list](https://www.prodpad.com/blog/best-product-management-books/).
