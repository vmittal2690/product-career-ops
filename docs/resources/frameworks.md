# Frameworks & Mental Models

Use one framework at a time, against a real decision. A framework that does not change
what you do is overhead.

## Discovery & customer understanding

- **Opportunity Solution Tree** (Teresa Torres) — connect a desired outcome to opportunities, solutions, and experiments. [Reference](https://www.producttalk.org/opportunity-solution-tree/).
- **Jobs to Be Done (JTBD)** — understand the progress a customer is trying to make; pair with Outcome-Driven Innovation (Tony Ulwick / Strategyn).
- **Continuous discovery** — weekly customer touchpoints feeding decisions, not a one-off research phase.
- **Double Diamond** (design) — diverge/converge across problem and solution spaces.

## Strategy

- **Diagnosis → Guiding Policy → Coherent Action** (Rumelt) — the kernel of good strategy.
- **Product operating model** (SVPG) — empowered teams, product vision, and outcome ownership.
- **Working Backwards / PR-FAQ** (Amazon) — write the press release and FAQ before building.
- **Three Horizons** — balance core, adjacent, and transformational bets across a portfolio.

## Prioritization

- **RICE** — Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort.
- **Kano model** — basic, performance, and delight needs.
- **Cost of Delay / WSJF** — sequence by economic urgency.
- **Opportunity scoring** — importance vs. satisfaction to find underserved needs.

## Goals & metrics

- **North Star Framework** (Amplitude / John Cutler) — one leading metric and its inputs. [Reference](https://amplitude.com/north-star).
- **OKRs** — objectives and measurable key results; pair with *Radical Focus*.
- **AARRR / Pirate Metrics** — acquisition, activation, retention, referral, revenue.
- **HEART** (Google) — Happiness, Engagement, Adoption, Retention, Task success.

## Selecting a framework

| Situation | Start with |
|---|---|
| Unclear customer problem | JTBD + Opportunity Solution Tree |
| Too many competing bets | RICE or Cost of Delay |
| Fuzzy strategy | Rumelt's kernel + Working Backwards |
| No shared definition of success | North Star + OKRs |
