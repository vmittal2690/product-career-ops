# Courses & Communities

Structured learning to close specific gaps, plus peer networks for ongoing calibration.

## Courses & programs

- **[Reforge](https://www.reforge.com)** — advanced programs on product strategy, growth, and leadership for experienced operators.
- **[SVPG workshops](https://www.svpg.com/workshops/)** — product operating model, coaching, and leadership, taught by the Cagan/SVPG partners.
- **[Product School](https://productschool.com)** — certifications across PM, leadership, and AI product management.
- **[Mind the Product training](https://www.mindtheproduct.com/training/)** — workshops on discovery, strategy, and leadership.
- **[Pendo / Productboard academies](https://www.productboard.com/product-makers/)** — free craft-focused content and communities.

## Communities

- **Lenny's Community** — large Slack community paired with the newsletter.
- **Mind the Product / ProductTank** — global meetups and an active community.
- **The Product Collective** — community and the INDUSTRY conference.
- **Women in Product** — community and events for women product leaders.
- **CPO Club / executive peer groups** — for VP+ and CPO-level peer exchange.

## How to choose a course

1. Name the capability gap precisely (from `develop skill-gap`).
2. Pick the *smallest* intervention that could close it (an essay or workshop before a multi-month program).
3. Define the outcome: what decision or behavior should change afterward.
4. Review in your next quarterly whether it did.
