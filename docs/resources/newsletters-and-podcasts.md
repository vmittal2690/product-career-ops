# Newsletters & Podcasts

High-signal, ongoing feeds. Subscribe to **3–5**, not 20 — the constraint is attention,
not access.

## Newsletters

- **[Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com)** — the most-cited PM newsletter; growth, strategy, careers, deep-dive interviews.
- **[SVPG / Silicon Valley Product Group](https://www.svpg.com)** — Marty Cagan and partners on the product operating model; essential for senior leaders.
- **[Product Talk](https://www.producttalk.org)** — Teresa Torres on continuous discovery and the opportunity solution tree.
- **[Mind the Product](https://www.mindtheproduct.com)** — broad PM craft, community, and events.
- **[Bring the Donuts](https://www.bringthedonuts.com)** — Ken Norton on product leadership and careers.
- **[The CPO Club](https://cpoclub.com)** — executive-level perspectives for CPOs, VPs, and directors managing larger orgs.
- **[Department of Product](https://www.departmentofproduct.com)** — trends, tooling, and tactical craft.

## Podcasts

- **Lenny's Podcast** — long-form interviews with product and growth leaders.
- **Product Thinking** (Melissa Perri) — strategy, operating models, and Q&A.
- **The Product Experience** (Mind the Product) — craft and leadership conversations.
- **Fearless Product Leadership** (Hope Gurion) — hiring, team structure, and stakeholder leadership.
- **This is Product Management** — applied interviews across domains.
- **a16z Podcast** — technology, markets, and (frequently) healthcare/bio and AI.

## Practice

Read/listen with a pen. For each piece, write one line: *what would I do differently?*
Bring the strongest of those into `develop coach` or your weekly review.
