# Fictional Job Posting: Senior Director of Product, Robotics Platform

**Company:** Kinetic Robotics  
**Location:** Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania or San Francisco, California  
**Work model:** Hybrid  
**Compensation:** Fictional base range of $260,000-$320,000 plus bonus and equity  

> Kinetic Robotics is a fictional company created for this demo. This is not a real job posting.

## The opportunity

Kinetic Robotics builds general-purpose mobile robots and a software platform that helps logistics and manufacturing teams deploy autonomous workflows. The Senior Director of Product will own platform strategy across fleet management, developer tools, autonomy services, analytics, and partner integrations.

## Responsibilities

- Set product vision, portfolio strategy, and investment priorities for the robotics platform.
- Manage a team of four product leaders.
- Partner with autonomy, hardware, cloud, safety, design, field operations, sales, and partnerships.
- Define customer segments, use cases, platform boundaries, and ecosystem strategy.
- Lead products from research transfer through launch and scaled deployment.
- Establish platform adoption, reliability, safety, and commercial metrics.
- Build relationships with integration partners and strategic customers.

## Qualifications

- 12+ years in product management, including five years leading product teams.
- Experience with robotics, autonomous systems, industrial software, or deeply technical hardware-software products.
- Strong platform and developer-product experience.
- Experience with enterprise deployment and partner ecosystems.
- Technical degree or equivalent applied experience.
- Ability to communicate technical tradeoffs to executives and customers.
