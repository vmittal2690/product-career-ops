# Fictional Job Posting: Vice President of Product, Clinical AI

**Company:** SignalCare AI  
**Location:** Boston, Massachusetts or remote  
**Work model:** Flexible hybrid  
**Compensation:** Fictional base range of $275,000-$335,000 plus bonus and equity  

> SignalCare AI is a fictional healthcare-AI company created for this demo. This is not a real job posting.

## The opportunity

SignalCare AI develops workflow and decision-support products for health systems. The Vice President of Product will lead product strategy, product management, design, and product operations while partnering closely with clinical, AI, engineering, security, regulatory, implementation, and commercial teams.

## Responsibilities

- Define company product strategy, portfolio priorities, and investment roadmap.
- Build and lead product management, design, research, and product operations.
- Turn health-system workflow problems into trustworthy AI products.
- Establish practices for model quality, human oversight, privacy, safety, adoption, and outcomes.
- Lead discovery, product development, enterprise launch, implementation, and expansion.
- Partner with the CEO and Board on company strategy and market positioning.
- Develop repeatable health-system co-development and product-validation models.

## Qualifications

- 12+ years in product management and at least five years leading product organizations.
- Experience launching enterprise healthcare or clinical products.
- Strong understanding of AI product development and regulated or high-trust workflows.
- Demonstrated product strategy, organization building, and executive influence.
- Experience with health-system implementation and enterprise go-to-market.
- Ability to balance clinical value, customer adoption, technical feasibility, and business outcomes.
