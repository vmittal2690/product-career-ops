# Fictional Job Posting: Head of Product, Member Experience

**Company:** Northstar Health  
**Location:** Remote within the United States  
**Work model:** Remote with quarterly travel  
**Compensation:** Fictional base range of $240,000-$285,000 plus equity  

> Northstar Health is a fictional digital-health company created for this demo. This is not a real job posting.

## The opportunity

Northstar Health helps people manage chronic conditions through virtual care, coaching, connected devices, and personalized software. We are seeking a Head of Product, Member Experience to lead the end-to-end member journey across onboarding, care navigation, engagement, outcomes, and retention.

The leader will manage product managers and partner with clinical, design, engineering, data science, operations, growth, legal, security, and commercial teams. The role owns product strategy and roadmap for the member experience and helps shape company-level portfolio and investment decisions.

## Responsibilities

- Define the multi-year product vision and roadmap for member experience.
- Build and develop a team of product managers.
- Translate member, clinician, employer, and health-plan needs into clear product priorities.
- Improve activation, sustained engagement, clinical outcomes, and retention.
- Partner with clinical and data-science leaders on personalized interventions and responsible AI features.
- Establish product metrics and experimentation practices.
- Lead discovery, delivery, launch, adoption, and ongoing optimization.
- Represent product strategy in executive and Board discussions.

## Qualifications

- 10+ years in product management, including leadership of product managers.
- Experience with consumer or member-facing digital products.
- Demonstrated zero-to-one and scaled-product experience.
- Strong analytical judgment and customer-research capability.
- Experience in healthcare, regulated industries, or high-trust products is strongly preferred.
- Ability to lead through influence across clinical, technical, operational, and commercial teams.
