# Evidence discipline

For every claim, capture:

- context
- problem
- user's role and decision authority
- actions
- measurable or observable result
- collaborators and stakeholders
- trade-offs
- reflection
- confidentiality level

Prefer exact evidence over adjectives. "Led 100+ engineering contributors" is stronger than "exceptional cross-functional leader."

Never turn adjacency into direct experience. Never invent a metric to make a story feel complete.
