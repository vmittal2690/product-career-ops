# Privacy and confidentiality

- Store only information needed for the user's career system.
- Never store secrets, passwords, tokens, patient data, surgeon-identifiable information, or confidential employer materials.
- Do not infer that internal product names, roadmaps, strategy, metrics, or regulatory details are public.
- Mark uncertain claims as requiring confirmation.
- Generated public exports must exclude the private workbook, personal source files, generated packets, backups, and audit history.
- Public export must scan for the user's name, emails, phone numbers, home paths, employer-sensitive terms, and common secret patterns.
- Fail closed if the scan finds a match.
